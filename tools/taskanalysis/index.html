How to use the Task Analysis Generator

This tool helps you build a Hierarchical Task Analysis (HTA) — breaking a user's overall goal into a sequence of observable steps.

  • Click + on any step card to insert a new step immediately after it.
  • Click ≈ to add a cognitive load note to a step — use this to flag where users must remember, decide, or switch focus.
  • Click × to delete a step. The first step cannot be removed.
  • Click on any card text to edit it directly.
  • The Goal card on the right always shows your original task. Lines connect each step to the next and finally to the goal.

Cognitive load notes appear in the lower section of a step card in a contrasting colour. Use them to identify: where users hesitate, make errors, or abandon a task.

Example loads a sample HTA. Clear resets to a single blank step. Download saves the canvas as a PNG image.
