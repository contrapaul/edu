3D Modeling

Students learn CAD tools to design and digitally prototype a product in response to a real-world design brief. The unit introduces parametric modelling, orthographic drawing conventions and the role of virtual prototyping in the design process.

Students identify a design opportunity, analyse existing products and their manufacturing methods, and produce a design brief that outlines user needs and product requirements.

Criterion A teacher notes and task guidance will be added here.

Students develop a design specification, generate multiple concept sketches, and refine a chosen design into detailed orthographic or CAD drawings ready for modelling.

Criterion B teacher notes and task guidance will be added here.

Students build the 3D model in CAD software, document the modelling process, demonstrate iterative refinement, and produce renders or technical drawings of the final model.

Criterion C teacher notes and task guidance will be added here.

Students evaluate the virtual model against the design specification, reflect on their CAD skills and design decisions, gather feedback, and propose improvements.
