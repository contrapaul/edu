Product Design

Statement of Inquiry: To be added from the G10 unit plan.

Students undertake an extended product design project that synthesises skills developed across the MYP programme. Working independently, they identify a genuine need, conduct in-depth research, design and fabricate a solution, and critically evaluate their process and outcomes in preparation for DP Design.

Extended product project 4 summative assessments (A–D) Suggested lesson sequence included

The unit moves through the four MYP design criteria in order, with each criterion assessed by its own summative task. The lesson sequence shown on this page is a suggestion, not a hard requirement — adjust pacing to fit the class. Phase details will be completed when the G10 unit documents are available.

Unit OverviewStudents follow the complete design cycle through an extended, independent product design project: identifying a genuine need and researching it in depth, planning with success criteria and specifications, designing and fabricating a solution, and critically evaluating the process and outcome in preparation for DP Design.
