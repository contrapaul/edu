Studio Project

Students pursue an open-ended, self-directed design project in a studio environment. With greater autonomy over their design context, they demonstrate mastery of the design cycle, make independent decisions about process and materials, and produce a portfolio-quality outcome that reflects their personal design voice.

Students independently identify a meaningful design context, conduct self-directed research, analyse precedents from professional design practice, and articulate a design brief that demonstrates genuine inquiry.

Students develop a rigorous design specification, explore a broad range of design directions, and document a clear, justified design development journey leading to a resolved final concept.
