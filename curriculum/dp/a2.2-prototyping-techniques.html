Prototyping Techniques

Guiding questionWhy is it necessary for designers to prototype ideas as part of a design process?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Prototyping spans low-fidelity sketches through to CAD virtual models and rapid-prototyped physical artefacts. These notes cover drawing conventions, fidelity levels, and manufacturing technologies.

Students must be able toExplain the advantages and disadvantages of using low- and high-fidelity prototyping within a design process.
