Introduction to Mechanical Systems

Guiding questionHow are mechanisms present in everyday products?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Mechanical systems convert motion and force through gears, pulleys, cams, levers and linkages. These notes cover the four types of motion, mechanical advantage, and how simple systems combine to create complex mechanisms.

Students must be able toIdentify the four basic types of mechanical motion: linear; rotary; oscillating; and reciprocating.
