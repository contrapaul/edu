The Design Process

Guiding questionHow do designers approach problem-solving?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

The five-stage design process — empathize, define, ideate and model, design a solution, present a solution — structures the practical design work in DP Design Technology. These notes cover primary and secondary research, persona development, storyboarding, product analysis, design specifications, iterative development and communication of solutions.

Students must be able toOutline each stage of the design process (empathize; defining the project; ideation and modelling; designing a solution; presenting a solution).
