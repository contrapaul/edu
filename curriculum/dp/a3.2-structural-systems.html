Introduction to Structural Systems

Guiding questionHow are structures present in everyday products?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Structural systems covers how forces act on and within objects — from frame, shell and solid structures through to stress-strain relationships, Young's Modulus, safety factors, and strengthening techniques.

Students must be able toAnalyse and interpret a variety of human-made and natural structures.
