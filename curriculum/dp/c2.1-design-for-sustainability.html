Design for Sustainability

Guiding questionHow can design for sustainability ensure we meet our current needs without compromising our future existence?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Design for sustainability covers Datchefski's five principles, the triple bottom line (people, profit, planet) and how designers balance competing priorities to reduce waste, pollution and energy consumption. These notes address each learning objective in turn.

Students must be able toDiscuss strategies to achieve sustainability and the importance of decision-making when addressing issues related to sustainable design, including waste, pollution and energy consumption.
