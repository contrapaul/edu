Introduction to Electronic Systems

Guiding questionHow are electronics present in everyday products?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Electronic systems span analogue and digital circuits, input/output components, microcontrollers, and embedded systems. These notes cover each learning objective in turn, from basic circuit concepts through to IoT and operational amplifiers.

Students must be able toDescribe an electronic system in terms of input, process, output and feedback.
