Design for a Circular Economy

Guiding questionHow do designers minimize waste and reduce product waste and pollution?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

The circular economy replaces the linear "take-make-dispose" model with closed-loop systems that eliminate waste through longevity, upgradability, disassembly, biodegradable materials and renewable energy. These notes address each learning objective in turn.

Students must be able toCompare and contrast a linear approach and the circular economy.
