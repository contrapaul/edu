Electronic Systems Application and Selection

Guiding questionHow can electronic systems be incorporated into product design?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Building on A3.4, this topic applies electronic systems to product design — measuring with apparatus, calculating V, I, R and P, designing flow diagrams and circuits, programming microcontrollers and integrating sensors and outputs. These notes address each learning objective in turn.

Students must be able toAnalyse simple electronic products and circuits to identify the main component parts that enable them to perform a specific function.
