Mechanical Systems Application and Selection

Guiding questionHow can mechanical systems be incorporated into product design?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Building on A3.3, this topic applies mechanical calculations to real design contexts — computing mechanical advantage, velocity ratio and efficiency, and constructing gear, cam and lever systems. These notes address each learning objective in turn.

Students must be able toCalculate mechanical advantage in gear, pulley, belt and lever systems.
