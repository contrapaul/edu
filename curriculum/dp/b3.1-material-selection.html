Material Selection

Guiding questionWhy is material selection crucial to designers of products?

Study GuideQuizPaper 2References

Material selection in Topic B moves from classification (A3.1) to applied decision-making — choosing materials based on properties, aesthetics, cost, availability and sustainability, and justifying those choices through research. These notes address each learning objective in turn.

Students must be able toIdentify appropriate materials based on their physical, chemical and mechanical properties.
